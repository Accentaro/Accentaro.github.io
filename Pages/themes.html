How to Install Themes

  1. Open Your Stand Folder:

    You can open it from the launchpad.

  2. Place Theme Files:

    Place the theme folder in \AppData\Roaming\Stand. Click okay to replace.

    Place the custom header folder in \AppData\Roaming\Stand\Headers. Click okay to replace custom headers.

    Place profile.txt in \AppData\Roaming\Stand\Profiles.

  3. Load the Theme:

    Once you've completed the above steps, load GTA and apply the profile.

  4. Keep Your Current Profile:

    If you wish to keep your current profile, copy this section of the profile from the theme you downloaded and replace yours with it and save:
