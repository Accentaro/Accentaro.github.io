Gifting Vehicles Guide

Gifting vehicles allows you to add spawned vehicles to your garage and summon them in the future without mods. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with a non-DLC 2-car or 10-car garage. Cheap ones near the airport work well. Apartment garages work, but NOT office, auto-shop, agency, bunker etc...
  2. On your phone, open Legendary Motorworks, click 2-door, Sort by Price, and select any FREE car. The Annis Eleghy RH8 should be free for all players. Select a color, purchase, and save to your garage.
  3. Repeat step #2 until the garage is completely full of cars. You can re-purchase the same free car over and over quickly without closing your phone, after purchase click back a few steps and then buy again.
  4. Walk inside the garage, and drive a car out and return it to the garage with the interact menu (m) to reset your personal vehicle.
  5. Spawn whatever car you want to keep (deluxo). Make sure it is upgraded how you want, paint colors, wheels, plate text etc...
  6. Do NOT be the lobby host when using the gift command (Players > Name > Friendly > Gift). If you (or giftee) get kicked from the car when you do this command, try NOT being the lobby host.
  7. Park the spawned car in garage and choose to replace one of the free cars.
  8. The old cars may end up as invisible vehicles outside the garage door. If this happens clear them away with a dune4 ramp buggy, or by other methods (deletegun, World Hacking, etc).
  9. Your new car may need insurance. This can be purchased from any LS Customs, or free from your own AutoShop.
  10. Most vehicles cannot be sold at all, or only for a minimal value. If selling for profit use a fully upgraded deathbike2 to sell for $1mil per. Limit to selling 2 vehicles per day for safety. (Note the deluxo no longer seems to be sellable ðŸ˜¦ )

Note: If for any reason you have tried all the steps, and it still didn't work, try a new session.
