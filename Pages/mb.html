How to Install and Use Musiness Banager

  1. Get Set Up with an Office and Warehouse:
    • Ensure you own an office to proceed with Musiness Banager.
    • Purchase a warehouse for "Special Cargo" from the SecuroServ computer in your office. Warehouses come in three sizes: small, medium, and large.
  2. Download the Script:
    • Access the script Here.
    • Wait for the gearwheel icon to change to a Checkmark, indicating that the script is installed successfully. Alternatively, you can navigate back one page or click here.
