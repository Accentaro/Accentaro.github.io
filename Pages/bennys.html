How to save custom wheels with Bennys

  1. Spawn your desired car using the vehicle spawner tool:
    • Vehicle Spawner
  2. Customize the car to your preference at Los Santos Customs using Stand:
    • Los Santos Customs
  3. Install Benny's Wheels from the "Wheels" category (category 7 or 8) at Los Santos Customs:
    • Wheels Category
  4. Enable "Disable LSC Restrictions" using the provided tool:
    • Disable LSC Restrictions
  5. Drive your customized car into Benny's Shop.
  6. Purchase full coverage and a tracker for the car.
  7. Choose and purchase your preferred tire design.
  8. Store the customized car at your desired location.
